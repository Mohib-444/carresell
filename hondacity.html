Honda City 2018

Overview: The 2018 Honda City is a stylish and reliable sedan known for its comfort, fuel efficiency, and sleek design.

Key Features:

  • 1.5L i-VTEC Petrol / i-DTEC Diesel Engine
  • CVT Automatic or 6-Speed Manual Transmission
  • Touchscreen Infotainment with Navigation
  • Rearview Camera and Parking Sensors
  • ABS with EBD and Dual Airbags
  • Premium Interior with Leather Upholstery (on higher variants)
  • Mileage: ~17-25 km/l (depending on fuel type and variant)
